b | read

is a book club. Of sorts.

We meet once a month. But rather than read a book, we bring a book. Any book is fine: fiction, nonfiction, poetry, a play, a screenplay, or even a TV show. But it must be, a book to be read.

We do have a few rules.

1. Bring a book

In form or in title. A book you've read - and want others to read.

2. Bring a bread

Plain, laminated, gluten-free, vegan, or not-even. Something to be eaten and shared.

3. Bring a self

As you are, as you were. A self - that listens and learns from another.
